Credits

All fonts are hosted at Adobe TypeKit.

The typeface used for the text is Alegreya, designed by Huerta Tipográfica. It is an Argentinian Type foundry that was founded in 2009, during the first year of the MA of Type Design in the University of Buenos Aires (CDT-UBA). It is formed by Juan Pablo del Peral, Carolina Giovagnoli, Sol Matas and Andrés Torresi, all of whom are Graphic and Type designers.

Headings uses Abril Titling Narrow, by TypeTogether. The owners, Veronika Burian and José Scaglione, met at the University of Reading whilst completing their MAs in Type Design, launching the independent type foundry TypeTogether in 2006.
