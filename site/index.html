It is an Argentinian Type foundry that was founded in 2009, during the first year of the MA of Type Design in the University of Buenos Aires (CDT-UBA). It is formed by Juan Pablo del Peral, Carolina Giovagnoli, Sol Matas and Andrés Torresi, all of whom are Graphic and Type designers.
