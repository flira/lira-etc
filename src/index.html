Bio

Fernando Lira is a designer who is concerned about the many aspects of user experience in websites, including accessibility, user feedback, information architecture and usability.

He obtained his bachelor degree in visual communications design in 2010, but since 2009 he continuously works as an UX designer - assisting and complementing other UI designers projects - and as a frontend developer. His main focus is to guarantee acessible, responsive and comprehensible UI coding, and consequently, great websites.

He also seems to like to talk about himself in the third person. Go figure.
